The five ".rom" files in this folder are:

48.rom - the image of original rom from ZX Spectrum 48K and ZX Spectrum plus (contains BASIC for 16/48K models)
128-english-0.rom, 128-english-1.rom - the images of original english roms from ZX Spectrum 128K (128K editor and menu and 48K BASIC)
plus2-english-0.rom, plus2-english-1.rom - the images of original roms from ZX Spectrum +2 (128K editor and menu and 48K BASIC)

See: https://sinclair.wiki.zxnet.co.uk/wiki/ROM_images
